About Me

Hi, I’m Ricardo Guerra, a New York based Software Engineer with a passion for building intuitive, user-focused applications that solve real-world problems. My journey into tech began with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from CUNY Queens College. Throughout my career, I developed a deep appreciation for analytical thinking and problem-solving. I channel that curiosity and determination into crafting seamless digital experiences. I specialize in full-stack development, with experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Python, Flask, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and SQLite. When I’m not coding, you can find me traveling or playing video games. I’m excited about using my skills to make a meaningful impact, whether it’s through innovative technology or by empowering others with tools to succeed. Let’s connect and create something amazing!

How Food Should Be

This is a webpage that displays images of the most mouth-watering foods imaginable. When a food image is clicked, the details for the food will be displayed in the section below the images including an enlarged image of the food, the name of the food, a description of the food, the name of the restaurant where the food came from, the location of the restaurant, a clickable link that takes you to the Google Maps location for the restaurant, and a clickable link that takes you to the Yelp page for the restaurant.
